Works

Automation of promissory notes control.

In this project, we were in charge of automating the control of private promissory notes through the development of a process consisting of obtaining data from scanned files, its subsequent collection and cleaning, and finally the display in tables related to the documents provided in a Power BI visualization.

Automation of Payment History control

Our team was responsible for automating the process of controlling bank payment histories for a well-known bank. The solution we designed was to create a process of data collection and cleansing, creation of alerts, debt evolution graphs and various points of interest for auditors and finally, its visualization through a series of graphs and tables presented in a Spotfire visualization.
